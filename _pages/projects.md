This page lists extensions to the core GATES model. It is currently under construction!
{: .notice}


## Multi-region training
Testing GATES across multiple world regions, assessing its transferability, and developing techniques for transfer learning.
[View Abstract](https://meetingorganizer.copernicus.org/EGU26/EGU26-20966.html)

## Background emulation
Developing a variant of GATES that can emulate background concentrations (GATES-BG), and testing the end-to-end ML-driven emissions inference pipeline.
[View Abstract](https://meetingorganizer.copernicus.org/EGU26/EGU26-22809.html)


## Prototypes
Increasing the model predictive capacity by adding a "prototype" to the inputs - an efficient approximation of the emulator's output. 
[View conference paper](https://www.climatechange.ai/papers/iclr2025/43)

## Uncertainty quantification
Quantifying uncertainty in footprint predictions, and integrating it into the emissions integration pipeline.
[View conference paper](https://bmva-archive.org.uk/bmvc/2025/assets/workshops/MVCC/Paper_5/paper.pdf) 

## Visualisation
Developing methods to present complex data in time and space, including footprint visualisation, meteorological data and methane retrievals.

View abstract on Unity-based TROPOMI visualisations [here](https://meetingorganizer.copernicus.org/EGU26/EGU26-20973.html).
